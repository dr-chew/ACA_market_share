## Final thoughts

The best way to deal with this is to build a model that incorporates county-level variation without over-fitting. This could be through a non-linear mixed-effect model, or a carefully constructed tree-based model.

Ultimately, however, much better models can be constructed with first-party data, which not only allows for metal-tier-level projections, but also delineation into separate models which project retention of existing members and acquisition of new members.
